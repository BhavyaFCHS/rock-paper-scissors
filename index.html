Rock Paper Scissors Game

Everyone knows the classic game rock paper scissors:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Once you start the round: click one of the following buttons to make your choice. The computer's choice is randomly generated.

Play

To play make your selection first, then press play round.

After 5 rounds, the winner is announced.
